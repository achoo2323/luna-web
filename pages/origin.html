Luna's Origin Story

How Luna Found Her New Owner

At 6 months old, Luna was abandoned by her then owner and thrown out onto the streets :(

The cat shelter found Luna and returned her to her owner but the owner said no I don't want her anymore.

So Luna lived in the shelter for a whole year and was about to be put down before a kind foster mum came to her rescue.

Her new official mum is less kind but all Luna had to do was headbutt her once and she was like wow give me this cat immediately.

Now Luna lives in luxury while her owner works hard to feed her.
